DFCAMCLP Portal

Admin Portal

The user begins here by signing in with their credentials.

The Admin dashboard displays all instructor records for review and management.

Clicking on More Details reveals the instructor's profile information and history.

On the Instructor Profile page, the Admin can update personal and professional details.

From the Home page, the Admin can access and submit payslips to the respective Instructor's Portal.

Under the Attendance tab, the Admin can track each instructorâ€™s daily logs.

The Attendance page shows real-time clock-in and clock-out entries.

The Leave Request page lists all submissions with their current approval status.

Leave requests are reviewed and either approved or disapproved by the Admin.

The Database page gives the Admin access to all instructor records and system data.

This section focuses on individual instructor profiles in the database.

Admins can delete inactive records, with confirmation prompts to prevent accidental deletion.

Instructor Portal

Instructors land on this Home page after logging in to access their tools and records.

Instructors can view and update their personal info via their profile page.

The Attendance page shows their clock-ins and clock-outs in real-time.

Instructors can submit and track the status of different types of leave.

The Payslip section lets instructors view and download their salary records.
