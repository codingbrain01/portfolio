DFCAMCLP Portal

Admin Portal

The user begins here by signing in with their credentials.

After logging in as an Admin, the dashboard displays all instructor records for review and management.

Clicking on More Details reveals the instructor's profile information and history.

On the Instructor Profile page, the Admin can update or modify personal and professional details.

From the Home page, the Admin can access and submit payslips to the respective Instructor's Portal.

Under the Attendance tab, the Admin can track each instructor’s daily attendance logs.

The Attendance page shows real-time clock-in and clock-out entries for instructors.

The Leave Request page lists all submitted requests, along with their current status—either approved or disapproved.

Leave requests are reviewed and approved or disapproved by the Admin.

The Database page provides the Admin with access to all instructor records and system data.

This section focuses on individual instructor profiles within the database.

Admins can delete inactive instructor records, but a confirmation prompt will appear before deletion to prevent accidental removal.

Instructor Portal

Instructors land on this Home page after logging in to access all their tools and records.

Similar to the Admin portal, instructors can view and update their personal information through their profile.

The Instructor Attendance page displays real-time entries of clock-ins and clock-outs.

On the Leave Request page, instructors can choose a date and type of leave—such as sick leave, special privilege leave, or vacation leave. They can also track the approval status of their requests.

The Payslip section displays records as soon as the Admin uploads them. Instructors have the option to download their payslips immediately.
